# Tokenmaxxing

Three essays on the meter, the firm, and the instrument.

**Live:** https://sudhanshuheda.github.io/tokenmaxxing/

1. **Part I — The Meter.** Token metering exists because nobody can measure the value AI creates across a firm's boundary. The meter is what you build when you cannot see output, and it stays profitable only while nobody can.
2. **Part II — The Firm.** A firm that reports tokens consumed as transformation is reading its productivity off the lab's invoice. The capacity AI frees is convex; revenue per FTE rewards amputating it. Manage by your own convexity instead of the lab's meter.
3. **Part III — The Instrument.** The durable application-layer business sells the measurement the labs cannot do, anchored to numbers the customer can audit without the vendor in the room, and leaves the humans at the edge intact.

The site is a single static HTML file (`index.html`), no build step. Markdown sources for the three parts are in [`parts/`](parts/).
